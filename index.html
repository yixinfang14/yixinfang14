Khaana Food Co. – Services

Welcome to Khaana! We offer simple and tasty food services for small businesses. Our goal is to provide reliable help at a fair price.

Services

  • Recipe Help – make simple menus that fit your budget (new).
  • Ingredient Sourcing – find fresh produce and common spices.
  • Food Safety Basics – labels and clean kitchen tips.
  • Delivery – local drop-offs on schedule available.

We try to keep things simple and clear for every order.

How It Works

  1. Talk about your idea and price.
  2. Make a small test and taste it.
  3. Buy ingredients and plan the work.
  4. Deliver and learn from feedback.
